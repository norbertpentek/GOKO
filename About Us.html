About Us

We are a construction and development company based in North London, specialising in high-end residential refurbishments and commercial maintenance. With over 12 years of experience, we offer comprehensive construction services, undertaking projects throughout North London and Hertfordshire. Go&Ko Services LTD was formed by two brothers-in-law, each bringing years of experience in the building trade. We are proud to have built a strong reputation in the area. Our approach to each project combines meticulous control and precision, ensuring the highest quality and attention to detail for all of our clients. Much of our work comes from introductions and referrals, allowing us to provide a personal service that guarantees exceptional workmanship. When you choose Go&Ko Services LTD for your project, you will work directly with us, benefiting from the best rates possible from suppliers for kitchens, tiles, wooden flooring, bi-fold doors, and sanitary ware. We collaborate with exceptional specialists, including architects and designers. Our use of professional tools minimizes dust and maximizes comfort, creating a cleaner environment for our employees and clients. We are committed to using less energy and producing less waste on each project, putting our clients and the environment first.
